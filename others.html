Our Other Services (其他服务项目)

We offer a number of other services, some of which are listed below.  Please call 0434955789 for enrolment and other enquiries.


Essays by Correspondence (作文邮寄批改)

This service allows students to email essays to us and have them marked by a teacher. 


Debating and Public Speaking Workshop (演讲辩论和口才)

- For Grades 5 to 8 

- Structured, fun guidance in both the speech writing and performing process

- Help to overcome nerves and stage fright 

- Let your child discover how to speak to persuade, enlighten and entertain! 


Interview Workshops  (面试技巧讲座) 

Has your child been invited by a school for an interview? 

We offer private workshops to help your child know the kinds of questions to expect, prepare materials for the interview, and increase their confidence to express themselves clearly.


Educational Consultancy (教育咨询) 

With our decades of expertise in all aspects of education, we may be able to offer you advice to assist you in achieving your child and family's goals.


Comprehending the Newspaper (报纸的阅读和理解)

This short course helps students understand common article formats and persuasive techniques used in newspapers. It allows them to familiarise themselves with the language used in newspapers so that they can navigate them with greater confidence.


Poetry - Appreciation and Writing (诗歌欣赏和创作)

This workshop enhances students' understanding of poetic language and devices. Students will explore different poem types and structures, before being given the  chance to write their own poems.


Short Story Study (短篇小说阅读和写作) 

Students will learn about literary devices, structure, plot, description, drama and character development.


Preparing for Mathematics Competitions  (各种数学竞赛辅导)

We offer workshops to prepare students for:

- Australian Mathematics Competition (AMC)

- Australian Intermediate Mathematics Olympiad (AIMO)

- Australian Mathematics Challenge and Enrichment

- Computational and Algorithmic Thinking (CAT)

- School Mathematics Competition
